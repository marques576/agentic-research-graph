comfortable with the vagueness of that assurance.

My recommendation: we accelerate the MT-7734-X filing with the USPTO. If we
can establish priority before any internal investigation at Harrington gains
traction, our position is substantially stronger. I would like to have the
patent application ready to file by November 1st.

I would prefer this memo not be circulated beyond yourself. The arrangement
with our contact has been financially beneficial but carries exposure I do not
