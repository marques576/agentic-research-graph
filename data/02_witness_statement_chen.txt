STATEMENT (verbatim transcript, edited for clarity)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

I have worked in the research laboratory at Harrington Pharmaceutical for four
years. My supervisor is Dr. Alan Frost. I work primarily with compound synthesis
and documentation for the clinical trials division.

On the evening of October 14th I was not in the building. I left at approximately
16:15. I can provide my bus pass as evidence; the 16:22 Route 9 bus is on my
regular schedule.

I did not have any interaction with Mr. Harrington on that day. I rarely had
direct contact with executive staff. I am sorry for his passing; he was considered
a fair employer.

Regarding the laboratory accident in April of this year: I was present in the
adjacent room when the solvent fire started. I smelled smoke and activated the
alarm. I do not know how the fire started. The accident investigation concluded
it was due to improperly stored solvents, which I accept.

I have no knowledge of any financial matters. I am not involved in procurement,
budgeting, or external vendor relationships.

I am not aware of any disputes or conflicts within the company.

I have nothing further to add.
